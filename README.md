# Spotify Music Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The Spotify Music Analytics Dashboard is a Power BI project designed to analyze music trends, genre popularity, artist performance, and the relationship between audio features and song popularity. The dashboard transforms raw Spotify data into meaningful insights through data cleaning, KPI development, and interactive visualizations.

---

## Project Objectives

* Identify the most popular genres.
* Identify the most popular artists.
* Determine the most popular songs.
* Analyze relationships between popularity and audio features.
* Build an interactive analytical dashboard.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning
* Data Visualization

---

## Dataset Information

**Dataset:** Spotify Tracks Dataset

* Total Songs: 113,999
* Total Artists: 31,428
* Total Genres: 114

The dataset contains information about songs, artists, genres, popularity scores, and audio characteristics such as Energy, Danceability, Valence, Tempo, and Duration.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Songs
* Total Artists
* Total Genres
* Average Popularity

### Analytical Visualizations

* Top 10 Genres by Average Popularity
* Top 10 Artists by Average Popularity
* Top 10 Most Popular Songs
* Energy vs Popularity Analysis
* Danceability vs Popularity Analysis
* Valence vs Popularity Analysis

---

## Key Insights

* Pop-film and K-pop emerged among the most popular genres.
* Artist collaborations frequently appeared among top-performing artists.
* Danceability demonstrated a moderate positive relationship with popularity.
* Energy alone did not strongly determine popularity.
* Popularity is influenced by multiple audio characteristics rather than a single factor.
